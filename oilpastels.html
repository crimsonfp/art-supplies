Oil Pastels

Quality Oil Pastels.



Sennelier // Mungyo // Holbien // Erengi // Faber Castell


Oil pastel, although a type of paint, is very different from acrylic. It is much slower drying, and has a completely different consistency. It's much thinner and less dense, and it carries more pigment than it's acrylic counterpart. Rather than water, oil pastel paint brushes must be cleaned with a special solvent. These containers come equipped with a silver whorlpool spring used to seperate the oil from the brushes. However, this solvent can be incredibly dangerous to humans, especially if inhaled for too long. Whenever you're cleaning oil pastel prushes, make sure to use gloves and not breath in the fumes for too long. Keep the lid on tight!
