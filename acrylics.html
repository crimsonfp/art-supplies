Acrylic Paints

Quality Acrylics Paints.



Meeden Art // Liquitex // Culture Hustle // Winsor & Newton // Blick



Acryclics are the most popular choice in paints. When you simply ask for 'paint' without specifying, you'll most likely recieve acyrlic. It's fast drying, making it easier to paint over mistakes. This is why acrylic is a great medium for beginners, as you can paint without fear of ruing the artpiece. Acrylic is also easy to mix, and any shade imaginable can be made by mixing out-of-the-tube paints. Brushes are also easy to clean, as acrylic can be washed off with water and a little bit of friction. If paint is particularly stuck to the brush, just soak for a few minutes to wash it off.
